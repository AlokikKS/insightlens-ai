# 🚀 InsightLens AI

> Transform customer feedback into actionable product decisions using AI.

InsightLens AI is an AI-powered Product Intelligence Platform that helps Product Managers quickly understand what customers are saying, identify recurring pain points, prioritize feature requests, and generate executive-ready product insights from thousands of customer reviews.

**🌐 Live Demo:** https://insights-ai-frontend-axg7.bolt.host

---

# The Problem

Product teams receive feedback from multiple sources—App Store reviews, customer surveys, NPS responses, support tickets, and user interviews.

Manually reading thousands of reviews is slow, inconsistent, and makes it difficult to identify the most impactful product improvements.

InsightLens AI reduces hours of manual analysis into minutes by automatically extracting meaningful insights using Large Language Models (LLMs).

---

# Solution

Upload a CSV containing customer reviews.

InsightLens AI analyzes the feedback and generates:

- Executive Summary
- Top Customer Complaints
- Top Praised Features
- Feature Requests
- Product Recommendations
- Executive PDF Report

Designed specifically for Product Managers, Founders and Product Teams.

# Product Thinking

Unlike traditional sentiment dashboards, InsightLens AI focuses on **decision-making**, not just analytics.

The platform answers questions such as:

- What should the product team prioritize next?
- Which complaints affect the largest number of customers?
- Which features are driving customer satisfaction?
- What quick wins can improve user experience?

The goal is to convert qualitative customer feedback into actionable product strategy.
